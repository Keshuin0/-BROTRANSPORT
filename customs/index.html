Frictionless Transborder Transit

Our automated compliance engine integrates directly with CBSA and CBP, verifying ACE and ACI manifests before your shipments reach the border. We eliminate delays with sub-minute electronic clearance processing.

// US-BOUND PIPELINE [PAPS / ACE]

Pre-Arrival Processing System

For commercial shipments entering the United States from Prince Edward Island, our engine coordinates standard Carrier Alpha Codes (BROP) and sequential Shipment Control Numbers (SCN) to compile automated manifest payloads.

  • Electronic manifest transmission > 1 hour prior to arrival
  • Direct integration with Automated Commercial Environment (ACE)
  • Pre-registered FAST Card commercial driver compliance
// CANADA-BOUND PIPELINE [PARS / ACI]

Pre-Arrival Review System

Northbound transits entering Canada are automatically processed via Advance Commercial Information (ACI) portals. We match commercial invoices to unique Cargo Control Numbers (CCN) prefixed with our carrier identification code.

  • Direct link to Canada Border Services Agency (CBSA) systems
  • Real-time webhook notifications via Release Notification System (RNS)
  • Automated broker entry auditing to prevent administrative penalties
